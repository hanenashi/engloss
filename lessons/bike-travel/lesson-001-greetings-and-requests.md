# Lesson 001: Greetings And Simple Requests

## Situation

You arrive in a small town on your bike. You need water, food, and a place to rest.

Your goal: greet people politely and ask for simple things.

## Useful Phrases

- Hello.
- Good morning.
- Good afternoon.
- Excuse me.
- Please.
- Thank you.
- I need water.
- I need food.
- I need help.
- Can I sit here?
- Can I charge my phone?
- Do you have water?
- Do you have a toilet?
- Where is the supermarket?

## Tiny Grammar

### I need...

Use `I need` for important things.

Examples:

- I need water.
- I need food.
- I need help.
- I need a map.

### Can I...?

Use `Can I` when you ask for permission.

Examples:

- Can I sit here?
- Can I charge my phone?
- Can I pay by card?

### Do you have...?

Use `Do you have` when you ask if something is available.

Examples:

- Do you have water?
- Do you have coffee?
- Do you have a room?
